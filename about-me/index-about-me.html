About Me

Hi and welcome to my portfolio site. My name is Saskia and I'm an aspiring Usability Engineer.

I started my academic career with a B.Sc. in Landscape and Environment management with a focus on marine ecology. After I graduated, I decided I was not done studying yet and took a gap year to explore my interests and take some time off. In the months that followed, I lived in Bonn, Germany and I learned German. During my first studies I was also interested in how humans interact with their environment and I have an innate interest for technology. During my specialisation in ecology, I encountered many systems that were eg. used to store sampling data. These systems were often hard to use and very cluttered. Then I found a great combination of my first and second studies, namely the design of systems for scientific purposes. I decided to pursue a second Bachelor's in User Experience Design and haven't regretted it one bit :)

I'm now in my fifth semester and preparing for my last year. I might do an internship at a medical company to do usability research for novel medical systems, but I'm not sure if it will work out yet.

A bit more about me: as you can see on the pictures, I'm a sports fanatic and you'll see me on a bike, board or skates almost every day. In the past, I have tested longboard decks and other components before they got launched. I have also participated in many races, for example marathons and 24-hour-races (solo and as a team).

After my Bachelors, I'm inclined to do a Masters in Human Computer Interaction and hope to inform you about it on my site!
